Sugared Campfire Donuts

Description

A quick and easy donut recipe for when you're out camping.

Ingredients

  • White sugar
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Biscuit dough
  • Melted butter

Steps

  1. Put the sugar and the cinnamon in a bowl and set it aside
  2. Roll the biscuit dough into balls
  3. Cook over the campfire, turn frequently until golden brown
  4. Dip into melted butter
  5. Roll in cinnamon sugar
