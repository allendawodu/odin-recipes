Ants on a Log

Description

Ants on a Log is a simple snack that anyone can enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Stalk celery
  • Peanut butter
  • Raisins

Steps

  1. Cut the celery stalks into bite size chunks
  2. Spread peanut butter on the top of the stalks
  3. Add evenly spaced raisins atop the peanut butter
